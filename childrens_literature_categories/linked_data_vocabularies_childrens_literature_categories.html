Categories for describing types of historical children's literature

RDF vocabulary derived from a thesaurus intended to provide terms for describing categories of historical children's literature. The thesaurus was constructed for use in describing the University of Washington Libraries' Historical Children's Literature Collection which can be seen at https://content.lib.washington.edu/childrensweb/index.html.
